About the Game

Ashes Beneath the Leaves is a dark fantasy adventure set in Elyndria, a land shaped by forgotten gods and fading magic. You play as Lucen Ardyn, a wanderer bound to an ancient ember that slowly awakens dormant shadows across the realm.

Explore enchanted forests, ruined citadels, and hidden temples as you uncover the truth behind Elyndria’s fractured past and shape the destiny of its fading light.
